My Mac Setup

Last Updated: Feb 5 2025

Device

I recently switched to a base M3 Macbook Air. The starlight color is pretty nice.

Settings

Always turn off the natural scrolling in the trackpad scroll if you ever plan to switch back and forth between using a mouse and the trackpad

Menu Bar tools

  • Maccy for clipboard
  • MonitorControl for brightness/volume control right from the menu bar
  • Rectangle for window management because new MacOS window management still isn't as good
  • Itsycal - cool little calendar widget
